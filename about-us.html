The Whole Story

At Lamar's Truck Sales, we are dedicated to providing high-quality, reliable used trucks that meet the unique needs of your business. Since our founding in 2021, we have successfully closed thousands of deals, becoming a trusted name in the used truck industry. Our focus is on offering 26ft box trucks that are perfect for a wide range of commercial applications, ensuring that each vehicle meets our rigorous standards for performance and durability.


Our Mission
Our mission at Lamar's Truck Sales is simple: to connect our customers with the trucks that drive their businesses forward. We understand that in the world of logistics and transportation, having a dependable truck can make all the difference. That's why we are committed to providing trucks that not only meet but exceed your expectations.


Why Choose Lamar's Truck Sales?

  • Expertise and Experience: With years of experience in the trucking industry, we know what it takes to keep your business moving. Our team of experts is here to guide you through the entire purchasing process, from selecting the right truck to securing financing and beyond.
  • Specialization in 26ft Box Trucks: We have honed our inventory to specialize in 26ft box trucks, making us the go-to source for businesses that require this specific type of vehicle. Whether you need a truck for local deliveries, moving services, or other commercial operations, we have a truck that will fit your needs.
  • Uncompromising Quality: Every truck in our inventory undergoes a comprehensive inspection and refurbishment process. We ensure that each vehicle is road-ready and built to withstand the demands of your business. Our trucks are not just used; they are revitalized to deliver optimal performance.
  • Competitive Pricing: We believe in providing great value for your investment. Our trucks are priced competitively, ensuring that you get the most out of your budget without sacrificing quality.
  • Flexible Financing Options: We understand that financing is a key component of your purchase decision. That's why we offer a variety of financing plans tailored to fit your financial situation. Our goal is to make the process as smooth and accessible as possible.
  • Customer-Centric Approach: At Lamar's Truck Sales, our customers are at the heart of everything we do. Our friendly and knowledgeable staff is dedicated to helping you find the perfect truck. We pride ourselves on our transparent and honest service, ensuring that you have all the information you need to make the best decision for your business.
